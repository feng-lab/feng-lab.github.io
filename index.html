    Welcome to the Feng Lab

    We develop novel image processing, computer vision, data mining and visualization algorithms to efficiently extract useful knowledge out of large-scale, heterogeneous bioimage data. We focus on developing bioimage informatics platform (e.g. neuTube ) for reconstructing and analyzing multiscale brain connectivity.

    We are looking for passionate new Postdocs, Graduate students, and Interns to join the team! If you are interested in working with us, please send me an email. State briefly why you are interested and attach a CV.
